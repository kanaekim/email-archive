What this means for you (and why it’s better)

With Metabase, you’ll be able to get more out of your AdvocateHub data with:

  • Better visualizations (cleaner charts, tables, and drill-downs) for exec-ready dashboards
  • Self-serve exploration (filter, segment, and iterate without needing custom help)
  • More consistent metrics so teams align on the same definitions
  • Strong data access controls so you only see data you’re meant to see
