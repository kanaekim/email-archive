Quick reminder:

Messenger 14.1.0 is now available

Dear Messenger Customer,

 

Just a quick follow-up to our earlier note about Aurea Messenger 14.1.0, now available across both MQ and ESB components.

This release introduces Java 17 support, important security updates, and platform improvements designed to support long-term stability in enterprise environments.

A quick reminder of why customers are upgrading

  • ☕ Alignment with Java 17 LTS, helping future-proof your Messenger deployment
  • 🔒 Security library updates addressing multiple CVEs
  • 🚀 Improved stability and compatibility across the platform and the Workbench

Messenger 14.1.0 remains backward-compatible with components dating back to version 10.0, enabling modernization of the core platform without disrupting existing integrations.

If you’re starting to think about upgrade timing or want to understand what this means for your specific setup, we’re happy to help.
