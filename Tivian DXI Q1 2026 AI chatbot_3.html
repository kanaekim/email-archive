Meet AI Chatbot for conversational feedback

We're excited to share a new capability now available in Tivian DXI — the AI Chatbot, designed to capture richer feedback through natural, conversational interactions.

 

What it does

  • Conversational data capture: Engage respondents through a dynamic chat experience instead of standard open-text fields
  • Two interaction modes: Free conversation (open chat) or questionnaire-style with optional answer choices
  • Survey-aware context: The chatbot can pull in relevant context from earlier survey responses to keep the dialogue consistent and on point
  • Export-ready output: Every conversation is delivered with a unique conversation ID and full transcript for seamless analysis and reporting
  • Configurable AI agent support: Works with supported AI providers (e.g., OpenAI, Cohere), based on your environment and configuration

How it's delivered

 

The AI Chatbot is offered as an advanced/custom question type, configured and installed by our team to match your specific use case and desired conversation flow.
