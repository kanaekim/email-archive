Tivian DXI: AI Chatbot for conversational feedback

The AI Chatbot is live in Tivian DXI. Capture deeper insights through conversation — have you tried it yet?

 

What it does

  • Conversational data capture: Engage respondents in a natural chat flow instead of traditional open-text questions.
  • Two modes of interaction:
    • Free conversation (open chat)
    • Questionnaire-style (guided questions with optional answer choices)
  • Survey-aware context: The chatbot can receive relevant context from earlier in the survey to keep the conversation consistent and informed.
  • Export-ready output: Conversations are delivered with a conversation ID and full transcript for analysis and reporting.
  • Configurable AI agent support: Setups can use supported AI providers (e.g., OpenAI, Cohere), depending on your configuration.

How it’s currently delivered

 

Today, the AI Chatbot is provided as an advanced/custom question type, configured and installed by our team based on your use case and desired conversation flow.
