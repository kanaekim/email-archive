Dear Tivian DXI Customer,

 

Over the past months, we’ve spoken with many customers to better understand what you need to succeed with Tivian. Based on this feedback, we’re excited to introduce two new capabilities that help you collect deeper, more actionable insights.

 

New Question Type: MaxDiff (Best–Worst Scaling)

The MaxDiff question type asks respondents to select the most and least important options from a list. This approach helps you clearly rank items by relative importance and uncover what truly matters to your audience.

 

Why use MaxDiff?

  • Prioritize product features, benefits, or messages
  • Reduce scale bias compared to traditional rating questions
  • Generate clear, decision-ready rankings

Example use case:
A service provider can identify which product attributes enterprise clients value most and focus development and marketing efforts accordingly.

 

AI Chatbot Question (Open-Ended Feedback)

With the new AI Chatbot question, participants can provide open-ended feedback by interacting with an intelligent chatbot instead of typing a static response.

The chatbot:

  • Understands the question context
  • Guides participants with follow-up prompts
  • Helps users articulate more detailed and meaningful answers

This results in richer qualitative feedback with less effort from respondents.
